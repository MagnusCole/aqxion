## üîß IMPLEMENTATION GUIDELINES FOR OTHER LLMS

### WHEN RECREATING THIS DESIGN:
1. **Start with problems, not solutions**
2. **Use questions as primary headlines**
3. **Maintain generous whitespace**
4. **Color-code by status (green=ready, amber=development)**
5. **Make cards clickable, not separate buttons**
6. **Keep animations subtle and purposeful**
7. **Prioritize mobile experience**
8. **Use emojis for quick visual scanning**

### AVOID THESE ANTI-PATTERNS:
- ‚ùå Complex gradients on buttons (use solid colors)
- ‚ùå Feature-first messaging (start with problems)
- ‚ùå Corporate jargon or technical complexity
- ‚ùå False urgency or sales pressure
- ‚ùå Cluttered layouts or overwhelming choices
- ‚ùå Promises about incomplete content

### SUCCESS METRICS:
- **Scannability**: User can understand value in 3 seconds
- **Natural flow**: Logical problem ‚Üí solution progression
- **Zero confusion**: Clear what's free vs. in development
- **Mobile-friendly**: Works perfectly on phone screens
- **Trust building**: Honest, transparent, helpful
